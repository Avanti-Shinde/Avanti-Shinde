Dhokla 
Ingredients: 
    

  • 1 1/2 cup besan / gram flour
    • 

  • 3 tbsp rava / semolina
    •           

  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
    •           

  • 2 chilli (finely chopped)
    •           

  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
    •     

  • 1 tsp sugar
    •           

  • A pinch hing / asafoetida
    •           

  • 1/2 tsp salt
    •           

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    •     

  • 1 tbsp oil
    • 

  • 1 cup water
    •       

  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
    •       

Directions: 
      

    • In a large mixing bowl sieve 1½ cup besan and 3 tbsp rava.
      • 

    • Add ½ tsp ginger paste, 2 chilli, ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp 
sugar, pinch hing, ½ tsp salt, 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp oil.
      • 

    • Prepare a smooth batter adding 1 cup of water or as required.
      • 

    • Additionally, add a pinch of baking soda. 

    • Immediately steam the dhokla batter for 20 minutes.
      • 

    • Further, cut the dhokla and pour tempering.
      • 

    • Garnish the dhokla with 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves and 2 
tbsp fresh grated coconut.
      • 

    • finally, serve the khaman dhokla with green chutney and 
tamarind chutney.
      •
